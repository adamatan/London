Orit Nissan-Messing: How to Go Serverless to Enable Faster and Simpler AI

Orit Nissan-Messing, Chief Architect and Co-Founder, Iguazio

Orit Nissan-Messing has vast experience in cloud architectures, storage, AI and big data. Prior to iguazio, Orit was Chief Architect at XIV (acquired by IBM) and held management roles in various companies from startups to corporations. Orit is a CNCF contributor and a member of the CNCF Serverless and Storage Groups.

Introducing serverless technologies which boost AI from development to production

The two most trending technologies today are AI and serverless and surprisingly enough, they even go well together. AI is shifting from batch processes to real-time, event-driven applications and serverless is taking things to the next level. This session will cover serverless technologies which boost AI from development to production, integrate event triggers and auto-scaling, support multiple coding languages and simplify package dependencies. Orit Nissan-Messing will demonstrate these technologies with cool AI use-cases such as sentiment analysis, image classification and predictive maintenance, and then dive into how to build a fast AI serverless app. You will learn how functions are built from a simple 'Hello World' through real-time stream processing, deep-learning and multi-stage workflows and how to distribute or update AI models in online systems, all with no infrastructure setup and only minimal coding.
